Alfajiri Cafe

Every sunrise deserves perfect harmony

Nestled in the heart of Kirinyaga's bustling Kutus ,mjini stage, Alfajiri cafe offers a warm and inviting escape from the town's fast-paced life.
Established in 2020, this charming cafe began as a small family-run coffee shop and has since grown into a beloved local gem. With its rustic wooden interiors, soft RnB, Soul and Gospel Music and the rich aroma of freshly brewed Kenyan coffee.
Alfajiri Cafe provides the perfect setting for casual meetups, remote work or quiet moments of reflection. Whether you are savoring a handcrafted espresso or indulging in their signature pastries, every bite tells a story of passion and sustainability, and every visit promises a delightfull experience steeped in history and comfort.
